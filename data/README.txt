The data in these directories are taken from open sources including:

* World Ocean Atlas 2009 Data in NetCDF format 
  NOAA National Oceanographic Data Center (NODC)
  http://www.nodc.noaa.gov/OC5/WOA09/pr_woa09.html

* ETOPO1 Global Relief Model 
  NOAA National Geophysical Data Center (NGDC)
  http://ngdc.noaa.gov/mgg/global/global.html
  Amante, C. and B. W. Eakins, ETOPO1 1 Arc-Minute Global Relief Model: 
  Procedures, Data Sources and Analysis. NOAA Technical Memorandum NESDIS 
  NGDC-24, 19 pp, March 2009. 

It is included here to support model testing.
